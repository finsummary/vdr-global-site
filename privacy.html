Privacy Policy

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date you first access our website or use our applications.

Introduction

VDR Global Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or use our mobile applications.

Website Privacy

This website does not collect personal data. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or any other data collection mechanisms on this website. Your visit to this website is completely anonymous.

Mobile Applications

Our mobile applications are designed with privacy in mind. The following describes our data practices:

Data We May Collect

Our applications may collect the following types of anonymized data:

  • Usage statistics (e.g., features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)

All data collected is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use Data

We use collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Understanding user behavior to enhance user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Anonymized data may be processed by third-party analytics services that help us understand app usage patterns. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and cannot identify individual users.

Data Storage and Security

Any data collected is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we may have collected
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your use of our applications
  • Opt out of data collection where applicable (check individual app settings)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
